Who are we?

We are the AENN, a nonprofit organization which has the aim of spreading knowledge about neuroscience field in Chile, considering that a formal neuroscience degree does not exist in our country. However, we hope thay someday neuroscience will be more relevant in education.

Origin of AENN

AENN's origin as a group of friends who were studying at university and shared a common interest for neuroscience. They wanted to show the lack of neursocience education in Chilean universities, as well as spreading scientific knowledge within student communities.

Values

Student Association of Neuroscience and Neurotechnology has five values that need to be respected in all activities:

To democratize knowledge

Scientific integrity

Gender equality

Respect to all people

Beneficence and not maleficence

Our mission

To build a community that generates interdisciplinar spaces about neuroscience and neurotechnology to facilitate access to these disciplines.

Our point of view

To be a national community of neuroscience and neurotechnology that interconnects all people in our country who are interested in these fields.

Our goal

To share knowledge regarding neuroscience and neurotechnology with students of diverse fields, through academic activities and scientific divulgation with the purpose of the democratization of knowledge and neuroscientific culture.
